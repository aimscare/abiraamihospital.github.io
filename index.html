About Us

We offer comprehensive consultation ,treatment services and orthopedic awareness for individuals suffering from musculoskeletal disorders. With our patient-centric focus and years of experience, we understand the challenges faced by our patients, So we provide expert guidance from consultation to rehabilitation.

At the Abiraami hospital we are dedicated to bring a complete, one-stop-solution to meet the needs of both local and overseas patients in a single, convenient location for additional peace of mind.

Our Specialities

Total Knee Replacement

Knee replacement, also known as knee arthroplasty, is a surgical procedure to replace the weight-bearing surfaces of the knee joint to relieve pain and disability.It is most commonly performed for osteoarthritis, and also for other knee diseases such as rheumatoid arthritis and psoriatic arthritis.

Total Hip Replacement

Hip replacement is a surgical procedure in which the hip joint is replaced by a prosthetic implant, that is, a hip prosthesis. Hip replacement surgery can be performed as a total replacement or a hemi replacement.

Arthroscopy

Arthroscopy is a minimally invasive surgical procedure on a joint in which an examination and sometimes treatment of damage is performed using an arthroscope, an endoscope that is inserted into the joint through a small incision.

Spinal Surgery

The bulk of spinal surgical procedures are split into degenerative conditions of the cervical (neck) and lumbar (back) spine, pediatric (child) and adult spinal deformities or scoliosis, spinal trauma, spinal infections, and spinal tumors.

Plastic & Reconstructive Surgery

Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery is a specialised surgical unit dealing with specific reconstructive and aesthetic problems. It provides services for congenital abnormalities, head and neck cancer, reconstructive microsurgery, cranio-maxillofacial surgery and trauma.

Microvascular Surgery

Microvascular surgery is used to reattach severed fingers, hands, arms, and another amputated parts to the body. This is done by reconnecting the small blood vessels, thus restoring circulation before the injured tissue begins to die. Microvascular is also used in reconstructive surgery.

Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery

Oral and maxillofacial surgery encompasses diagnosis, surgical and related management of various conditions such as impacted/embedded teeth (particularly wisdom teeth), misaligned jaws, facial pain and trauma.

Brachial Plexus Surgery

Brachial plexus injury refers to any damage to the bundle of connected nerves in the neck region of your spinal cord.Brachial plexus injuries surgery generally consists of a combination of nerve grafting and nerve transfer procedures. The treatment for each patient will be determined according to his or her individual condition.

Diabetic Foot Ulcer Surgery

Many non-infected foot ulcers are treatable without surgery. However, surgery may be required to remove pressure on the affected area including shaving or excision of bone(s),correct deformities, such as hammertoes, bunions, or bony “bumps.” and to treat infections such as osteomyelitis, an infection of the bone, by surgically removing the infected bone.
